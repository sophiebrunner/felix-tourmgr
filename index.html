About

Moin, I'm Felix. Nice to meet you. I'm a freelance tour and arts manager from Hanover and Hamburg, Germany.

One of my current clients is Belgian Singer-Songwriter MILOW. I accompany him to all his tour dates as well as radio and television appearances.

Before I started my own business I worked in the administration of the NDR Radiophilharmonie in Hanover, Germany. My responsibilities were all internal and external communication to agencies, festivals and promoters, artist assistance and support as well as video and social media content.

Already before and during my studies in Musicology and Arts Management at the University of Music Franz Liszt Weimar I was able to gain professional experience as project and tour manager for several choirs I was singing with. Furthermore, Kunstfest Weimar and Kunstfestspiele Herrenhausen asked me to take responsibility for differenct projects in several festival seasons.
