About me

Currently I am working for MILOW. I manage his tours and accompany him to radio and television appearances (ARD, ZDF, FFH, 104.6 RTL).

Before I started my own business as an arts and tour manager, I worked for four years at the NDR in the artistic administration of the NDR Radio Philharmonic.

I have a Bachelor of Arts in Musicology and arts adiministration from the University of Music Franz Liszt Weimar and eight years of experience as a project manager at festivals and on concert tours.
